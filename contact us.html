Frequently Asked Questions

What are your store hours?
Our store in Namugongo, Kampala is open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We’re closed on Sundays.

Currently, we ship within Uganda only. We’re working on expanding our shipping options soon!
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, we’ll send you a tracking link via email or WhatsApp.
